Get on the list for your chance to see the 2018 RLX first.
As promised, we’re excited to share more details with you about the new RLX — our most sophisticated and best performing luxury sedan ever. Elegantly restyled inside and out, the 2018 RLX features a sleek new design inspired by the Acura Precision Concept to complement its jaw dropping performance capabilities.
Two Powerful Choices
The 2018 RLX is engineered to excite with two outstanding powertrains. Whether you choose the 377-HP Sport Hybrid with 7-Speed Dual Clutch Transmission or the 310-HP Precision All-Wheel Steer™ with an all-new 10-Speed Automatic Transmission, a thrilling driving experience awaits.
Substance Meets Style
Impressive new design details add sporting character to the 2018 RLX. Just a few performance-inspired touches include our signature Diamond Pentagon Grille, restyled LED taillights, dual exhaust finishers, and a gloss black rear diffuser. The sedan’s class-leading space and comfort have also been elevated with redesigned sports seats featuring high-contrast piping and stitching.
Safety Comes Standard
Arriving in style is important, but getting there safely is a must. That's why the new RLX comes equipped with AcuraWatch™, a suite of safety and driver assistive technologies that can even help you avoid collisions. The all-new Traffic Jam Assist, an Acura first, is also standard on the new RLX. Together with adaptive cruise control and low speed follow, Traffic Jam Assist helps keep you in your lane in highly congested traffic with little or no input required.
