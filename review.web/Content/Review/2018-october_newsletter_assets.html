ARX-05 Prototype Ready to Race
We’re excited to share that Acura is returning to prototype racing in partnership with the legendary Team Penske. The Acura ARX-05 (Acura Racing eXperimental, generation 5) is the latest in an impressive line of endurance prototypes we’ve fielded since 1991. Our new race car debuts at the season-opening Rolex 24 in January, 2018, and will be driven by winning INDYCAR racer Helio Castroneves along with teammates Juan Pablo Montoya and Dane Cameron.
