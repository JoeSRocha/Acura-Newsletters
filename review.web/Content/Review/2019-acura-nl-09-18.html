Acura holds the distinction of being the world’s first Japanese automotive luxury brand. KING OF THE HILL
When we say that racing’s in our blood, we mean it. Acura Motorsports recently set three course records at the 2018 Pikes Peak International Hill Climb, a.k.a “Race to the Clouds.” NSX earned a new hybrid record; TLX GT won the Open Class with a record-setting run; and TLX A-Spec set a new FWD record.
 
 
The 2019 Acura RDX also scored a podium finish in its race debut. Congratulations to our racing teams for bringing the speed!
