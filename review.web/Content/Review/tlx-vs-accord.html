V–6 POWER
Featuring an available 3.5-liter i-VTEC V-6 with 290 horses, the new TLX packs a serious punch.
A smooth-shifting 9-speed automatic transmission elevates your daily drive. And when you’re ready to take command, race-inspired paddle shifters are always at your fingertips.
SUPERCAR DNA
Engineered to excite, the new TLX boasts groundbreaking innovations found in the iconic NSX including the Integrated Dynamics System that offers four unique driving modes at the touch of a button.
Available torque-vectoring Super Handling All-Wheel Drive™ – also a hallmark of the NSX supercar – delivers supreme handling and control.
INTUITIVE COCKPIT
A powerful and precise driving experience is only part of the TLX story. Every performance-inspired detail in the new TLX cockpit is also intuitive and accessible, creating a truly personalized space.
Apple CarPlay™ and Android Auto™ keep you connected to texts, emails and music, and a wireless charging pad keeps your smartphone powered. Heated front and rear seats are especially nice options to explore.
NEXT LEVEL A-SPEC®
Our best A-SPEC package ever is ready for the new TLX. Distinguish your ride with performance-driven features like a signature grille with dark-chrome surround and gunmetal mesh, oval fog lights, piano-black rear spoiler, exclusive red or black-trimmed interior options, and red LED ambient lighting.
More than just a sportier design, the A-SPEC package is also engineered to attack the turns with exclusive 19-inch Shark Gray Split five-spoke wheels, road-grabbing 245-section tires, retuned steering, and upgraded suspension.
