The New 2018 TLX
The bold and alluring new look of the 2018 TLX was on full display in your first exclusive email (check out a recap below if you missed it). Now it's time for a sneak peek inside the cabin to understand why true performance is not just horsepower, but the exhilaration of feeling effortlessly connected to your car.
 
Stylish Cabin
Beautifully crafted surfaces, intricate design details and available ambient lighting take the new TLX interior to another level.
 
Intuitive Cockpit
Your own personal sanctuary, every performance-inspired detail in the TLX cockpit is intuitive and accessible. Thoughtfully designed to support the driver and front passenger, it boasts a center stack with two display screens – one for phone and navigation, and another for audio and climate controls. Apple CarPlay™ and Android Audio™ are also supported, keeping you connected to everything you love about your smartphone.
Premium Seating
The A-SPEC available premium red leather sport seats with detailed stitching and piping deliver exceptional comfort and style. Heated front and rear seats are also nice options to have, especially on chilly mornings.
Stunning Audio
Music to the ears for anyone who loves good tunes, the new TLX features the critically acclaimed ELS Studio® Premium Audio System. With music sources available over the air, internet or your own digital device, your next road trip will be a symphony in motion.
AcuraWatch Assurance
The new TLX is loaded with advanced safety features including AcuraWatch™, a suite of driver assistive technologies that continuously monitor the vehicle's surroundings to help you avoid accidents.
New Features
With available features like wireless cell phone charging, Surround-View Camera System, four-door Smart Entry, and a heated steering wheel, the new TLX elevates connectivity and comfort.
Next Level A-SPEC® Package
Enhance your new TLX with performance-driven styling, retuned steering, and upgraded suspension.
Apple Carplay™ & Android Audio™
Seamlessly and safely integrate your smartphone into the TLX's touchscreen display.
Refreshed Fascia & Grille
Turn heads with a boldly refreshed front and new diamond pentagon grille.
Jewel Eye® LEDS
Enjoy stylish new headlights that burn whiter, brighter and last longer.
New Wheels
Choose the perfect look from a variety of exciting new alloy wheel styles and sizes.
