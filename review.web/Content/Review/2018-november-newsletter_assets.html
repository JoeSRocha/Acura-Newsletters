READY TO ROAR
The 2018 racing season was one to remember with 14 podium finishes, 3 team wins, and 2 championship fights that went down to the wire. Outstanding performances were delivered off the track as well, notably record-setting hill climbs by legendary Acura driver Peter Cunningham at Pikes Peak and Empire.
 
With last season now in the rearview, Acura Motorsports has its sights set on the 2019 IMSA WeatherTech SportsCar Championship. Team Penske will once again compete in the Prototype class with the Acura ARX-05, while Michael Shank Racing will field the NSX GT3 in the GT Daytona class. Heinricher Racing has also announced a partnership with Meyer Shank Racing and Caterpillar to compete in the #57 Acura NSX GT3 with a groundbreaking, all-female race team.
January 4th-6th marks the unofficial start of the racing season at The Roar Before the Rolex 24. Acura Motorsports is gearing up now for this three-day event at Daytona International Speedway, which serves as the final dress rehearsal before the 2019 season-opening Rolex 24 at Daytona on January 26th.
