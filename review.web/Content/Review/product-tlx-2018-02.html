The New 2018 TLX
As promised, we’re thrilled to give you an exclusive first look at our next-level performance sedan, revealed just today at the New York Auto Show! Inspired by the aggressive and alluring look of the Acura Precision Concept, the new 2018 TLX is the future of luxury performance — and it’s well within your reach.
Now onto the good stuff! Check out some of the awesome exterior enhancements on the new 2018 TLX below. We’ll highlight new interior features in your next exclusive email!
Next Level A-SPEC® Package
Our best A-SPEC package ever is ready for the new TLX. Take your ride to the next level with performance-driven details like a signature grille with dark-chrome surround and gunmetal mesh, oval fog lights, piano-black rear spoiler, exclusive red or black-trimmed interior options, red LED ambient lighting, special badging and more.
More than just a sportier design, the A-SPEC package is also engineered to attack the turns with exclusive 19-inch Shark Gray Split five-spoke wheels, road-grabbing 245-section tires, retuned steering, and upgraded suspension.
Apple Carplay™ & Android Audio™     
Everything you love about your smartphone – from maps and music to texts and emails – seamlessly and safely integrates into the TLX’s touchscreen display. You’ll always stay connected, wherever the road takes you.
