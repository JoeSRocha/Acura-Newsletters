BUILD & PRICE YOUR TLX
Whether you choose a high-revving, 206-hp inline-4 or a 3.5-liter i-VTEC V-6 with 290 horses, the TLX packs a powerful punch. And when you’re ready to take command, race-inspired paddle shifters are right at your fingertips.
Engineered to excite, the TLX boasts groundbreaking innovations found in the iconic NSX including the Dual-Clutch Transmission on 2.4L models.
The Integrated Dynamics System, found in all TLX models, offers four unique driving modes at the touch of a button. Available torque-vectoring Super Handling All-Wheel Drive™ – also a hallmark of the NSX supercar – delivers supreme handling and control.
Every performance-inspired detail in the TLX cockpit is also intuitive and accessible, creating a truly personalized space. Apple CarPlay™ and Android Auto™ keep you connected to texts, emails and music, and a wireless charging pad keeps your smartphone powered. Heated front and rear seats are especially nice options to explore.
