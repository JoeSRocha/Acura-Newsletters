As promised, we’re thrilled to give you an exclusive first look at the all new Acura RDX Prototype, just revealed at the 2018 North American International Auto Show.
Powerfully influenced by the Acura Precision Concept and the Acura Precision Cockpit, the RDX Prototype showcases our most extensive redesign in more than a decade. This next-level luxury SUV has also been entirely designed, developed and manufactured in America.

DYNAMIC NEW DESIGN
Exterior styling mirrors the low, wide and sleek presence of the Acura Precision Concept, and displays strong character lines that run along the entire profile of the vehicle.
The dynamic new design flows outward from the SUV’s signature diamond pentagon grille to its sharply sculpted body featuring all-LED exterior lighting.
Spacious and Luxurious Cabin
Featuring key design elements from the Acura Precision Cockpit, the RDX Prototype boasts a stunning, tech-savvy interior with floating center console and all-new touchpad interface and operating system.
A longer wheelbase provides class-leading cabin and cargo space. All 2019 RDX models will also come equipped with an ultra-wide panoramic sliding moonroof, largest in the class.
AcuraWatch™ Assurance
The new RDX will also come equipped with AcuraWatch™, a suite of driver assistive technologies that can even intervene to help you avoid collisions.
Additional connected-car features will be available as well including front and rear parking sensors, Rear Cross Traffic Monitor and Blind Spot Information System.
