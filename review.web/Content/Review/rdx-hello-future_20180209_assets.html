POWERFUL AND PRECISE
Beyond its evocative new styling, the RDX Prototype brings power and precision to your daily drive.
A new 2.0L DOHC VTEC® Turbo direct-injected engine gets your heart racing while a segment-first 10-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp and refined shifts.
A MODE FOR EVERY MOOD
When you're ready to change things up, the Integrated Dynamics System offers four exhilarating driving modes at the touch of a button: Comfort, Sport, Sport+, and Snow.
This innovative driving system is also shared with the iconic NSX supercar.
INTUITIVE TECH
The RDX Prototype claims the industry's most intuitive audio and information control system, accessible via a 10.2-inch full-HD display.
A new natural language voice recognition system also dramatically improves the ease of voice commands in the vehicle.
AMAZING AUDIO
Love good tunes? The RDX Prototype features the 16-channel ELS Studio 3D system developed by Panasonic and tuned by a Grammy-winning music producer.
Four ultra-thin, ceiling-mounted speakers also add a new dimension of sound and fidelity to your audio experience.
AMERICAN BORN & BRED
The RDX Prototype will be built with heart in the heartland. Development of this next-level SUV was led by our Ohio-based R&D team, with styling by the Acura Design Studio in Los Angeles.
Production will take place in our East Liberty, Ohio plant while the engines and transmissions will be manufactured in Ohio and Georgia.
