DYNAMIC NEW DESIGN
Exterior styling on the new RDX mirrors the low, wide and sleek presence of the Acura Precision Concept. The dynamic new design flows outward from the SUV's signature diamond pentagon grille to its sharply sculpted body featuring all-LED exterior lighting.
SPACIOUS AND LUXURIOUS CABIN
The new RDX boasts a stunning, tech-savvy interior with floating center console and all-new touchpad interface and operating system.
A longer wheelbase provides class-leading cabin and cargo space. All 2019 RDX models will also come equipped with an ultra-wide panoramic sliding moonroof, largest in the class.
POWERFUL AND PRECISE
Beyond its evocative new styling, the new RDX brings power and precision to your daily drive. A new 2.0L DOHC VTEC® Turbo direct-injected engine gets your heart racing while a segment-first 10-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp and refined shifts.
AMERICAN BORN & BRED
The All-new RDX will be built with heart in the heartland. Development of this next-level SUV was led by our Ohio-based R&D team, with styling by the Acura Design Studio in
Los Angeles.
Production will take place in our East Liberty, Ohio plant while the engines and transmissions will be manufactured in the states of Ohio and Georgia.
