The 2019 MDX takes luxury to the next level. Standard leather seats, a premium interior trimmed in handcrafted natural wood, and front row seating with 4-way power lumbar adjustments are just a few of the cockpit enhancements you’ll enjoy.
A new second-row seat configuration is also available, giving your passengers easy access to the third row.
With over 90 cubic feet1 of available cargo space, there’s plenty of room for groceries and gear. Second– and third–row seats can also be easily configured to meet a variety of passenger and storage needs.
Apple CarPlay™ and Android Auto™ Integration is standard on all MDX models, keeping you connected to texts, emails and music. And with up to seven USB ports available throughout the SUV, passengers in every row can charge electronic devices.
An impressive 290-HP i-VTEC® V-6 gives the MDX plenty of passing power without sacrificing fuel economy. Super Handling All-Wheel Drive™ (SH-AWD®) with innovative torque vectoring is also available for even greater handling and control.
Our philosophy is simple – help protect everyone. That’s why AcuraWatch™ comes standard on every MDX. Our suite of innovative safety and driver assistance technologies – from Collision Mitigation Braking to Lane Keeping Assist – brings added peace of mind to your daily drive.
Ready to really stand out? The MDX A-Spec® features head-turning touches like 20-inch Shark Grey alloy wheels, chrome dual exhaust outlets, and sport seats trimmed in rich red or black leather.
