SUPER HANDLING ALL-WHEEL DRIVE®
Available on the new RLX Sport Hybrid, our revolutionary Super Handling All-Wheel Drive (SH-AWD) system gives you an incredible connection with the road. The system uses 3 electric motors paired with a highly potent V-6 to send power directly to the wheels that need it.
The result? Lightening quick acceleration, increased traction on slippery roads and ultra precise control. A similar pulse-raising system powers the NSX supercar.
PRECISION ALL-WHEEL STEER®
The world’s first Precision All-Wheel Steer (P-AWS) system provides heightened control and handling. Available on the new 310-horsepower Acura RLX, this intelligent system precisely adjusts the angle of each rear wheel independently for improved stability while cornering, changing lanes or braking. It also gives the new RLX more maneuverability in tight spots.
SPORTSHIFT PADDLE SHIFTERS
Precise and intuitive automatic shifting is a hallmark of the new RLX. But when it’s time to let loose, Sequential SportShift Paddle Shifters offer instant control at your fingertips.
